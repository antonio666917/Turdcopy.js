Turdcopy.js

This small Javascript plugin's primary goal is to let non-coders who are afraid of seeing pages full of code be able to edit text strings on websites in an intuitive way.

View demo »

Why?

"Can you fix this typo real quick?" is often a question developers dread. When deadlines are tight, we cannot afford wasting our limited time on these small things when coding is to be done. Getting these small but repetitive requests saves us time and our employers money.

How?

Even with our limited time for this Hackathon we were able to develop something easily pluggable that works with minimal configuration.

Where?

To see it in action, visit the demo site we built to present its capabilities.
